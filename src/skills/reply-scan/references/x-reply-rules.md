# X Reply Platform Rules

## Character Limit
- Maximum 280 characters per reply. This is non-negotiable.

## Prohibited in Replies
- No links of any kind (URLs look spammy in replies)
- No hashtags (they hurt reach in 2026)
- No emojis overload (max 1-2 if any)

## Tone
- Conversational and authentic
- Opinionated with a clear point of view
- First person ("I", "we", "my")
- Sound human — avoid corporate language, marketing speak, or AI-sounding phrases
